**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



As college students, we spend a lot of time online for studies, social media, and online payments. It is important to follow some safety practices to avoid cybercrime.



**Prevention Tips**

* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
* Enable two-factor authentication on email, social media, and banking apps.
* Do not share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
* Always verify links before clicking, especially in emails or messages.
* Do not accept friend requests from unknown people on social media.
* For UPI safety: Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money.
* For UPI safety: Never approve “Collect Request” from unknown people.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or online payments.
* Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security updates.
* Log out from accounts when using shared computers.



**Reporting Cybercrime**



If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:



National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930



Reporting quickly can help prevent further financial loss and help authorities take action.
