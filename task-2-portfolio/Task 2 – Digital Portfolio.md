**Task 2 – Digital Portfolio** 







In Task 2, I created my digital portfolio by making profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Before this task, I had heard about these platforms but never actually created profiles on them. While setting up my profiles, I understood how important it is to maintain a professional online presence as a student. GitHub can be used to upload coding projects and track progress, LinkedIn helps in building a professional network and exploring internships and job opportunities, and Kaggle can be used to practice programming and data science skills.



This task helped me realize that building a digital portfolio is important from the first year of college because it shows our skills, projects, and learning progress. In the future, I plan to upload my projects on GitHub regularly, improve my LinkedIn profile, and participate in coding or data science activities on Kaggle. This task helped me understand the importance of digital identity and professional presence online
