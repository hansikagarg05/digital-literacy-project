**Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic** 

For Task 1, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The purpose of this infographic was to explain the importance of digital literacy to students in a simple and visual way. In the infographic, I included topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. I tried to keep the information short, clear, and easy to understand so that anyone looking at the infographic could quickly learn the basic concepts of digital literacy.



While making the infographic, I explored different templates, icons, colours, and layouts. At first, it was a little difficult to decide how to arrange the content properly without making the infographic look too crowded. However, after trying different layouts, I was able to organize the information in a neat and visually appealing way. This task helped me understand how to present information creatively using digital tools, and I also learned that visual communication is an important part of digital literacy.
