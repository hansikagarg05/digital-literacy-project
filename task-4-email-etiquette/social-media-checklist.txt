  Do’s
Do use social media responsibly and respectfully.
Do protect your personal information and privacy.
Do verify information before sharing it online.
Do use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.
Do maintain a positive and professional online presence.
Do respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.
Do use strong passwords and security settings.
Do report inappropriate or harmful content.
Don’ts
Don’t share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords.
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Don’t believe or share fake news without verification.
Don’t spend too much time on social media and neglect studies.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people.
Don’t click on suspicious links or messages.
Don’t post anything that can harm your reputation.
