**Task 4 – Email Etiquette \& Responsible Social Media Use**







In Task 4, I learned about email etiquette and responsible digital communication. I wrote one formal email and one informal email to understand the difference between professional and casual communication. The formal email included a proper subject line, greeting, polite language, and professional closing, while the informal email used simple and friendly language. This helped me understand how communication style changes depending on the situation and the person we are writing to.



I also created a list of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media use as a college student. Through this task, I understood the importance of protecting personal information, avoiding fake news, using respectful language online, and maintaining a positive digital identity. This task helped me understand that our online behavior is very important and should always be responsible and respectful.
